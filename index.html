Founded in 2001, Sunrise School has established itself as a premier educational institution in Somalia. Our holistic approach to education balances academic rigor with character development and extracurricular enrichment.

We take pride in our:

  • Highly qualified and dedicated teaching staff
  • Comprehensive curriculum that meets international standards
  • Safe and inclusive learning environment
  • Focus on STEM education and creative arts
  • Strong community partnerships

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds who demonstrate academic potential and a commitment to our values. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and supportive.

Steps to Apply

  1. Inquiry: Contact our admissions office or attend an open house
  2. Application: Complete the online or paper application form
  3. Assessment: Age-appropriate evaluation for placement
  4. Interview: Family meeting with school administrators
  5. Decision: Notification within 2 weeks of completing all steps
